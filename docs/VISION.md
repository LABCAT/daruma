# Vision — Project Daruma

## The Mission

Build a portfolio of 100 micro apps and games across web and mobile, using AI to accelerate development and iteration. Generate compounding passive revenue through ads, IAP, and small monthly subscriptions.

The founder is the CEO — providing creative direction and ideas. AI is the workforce — building, researching, iterating.

## The 100 Apps Goal

Not a single big bet. Many small bets. Ship fast, find signal, double down on winners, kill losers. The goal is not app 100 — it is what is learned getting there.

## The Four Things Being Learned in Parallel

1. **Product** — what do people actually want and use
2. **Distribution** — ASO, SEO (founder builds; Reddit/PH deferred to Daruma automation)
3. **Monetisation** — ads, IAP, subscriptions, one-time purchases
4. **AI Leverage** — using AI to build faster and faster each iteration

## Phases

### Apps 1–10: Learn the Pipeline
- Full deployment, monetisation, ASO, ad integration
- Revenue near zero — that is fine
- Goal: touch every part of the system once

### Apps 11–30: Recognise Patterns
- Double down on what works
- Revenue starts to trickle
- Prompt library and templates maturing

### Apps 31–60: System Running
- AI generating variations faster
- Revenue compounding
- Templates cloned not rebuilt

### Apps 61–100: Portfolio Business
- Optimising not just building
- Some apps die, some grow
- Meaningful passive income: $500–$2k/mo; long-term north star ~$100k/year profit (replace day job)

## Revenue Targets

| Milestone | Timeframe |
|-----------|-----------|
| First $1 of revenue | 1–3 months |
| 10 apps live | 3–6 months |
| First $500/mo | 6–12 months — momentum milestone; proof the model works |
| $500–$2k/mo passive income | 12–18 months |
| ~$100k/year profit | Long-term — full-time portfolio income |
| 100 apps | 2–3 years |

$500/mo is the "it's working" signal. Once revenue is real, reinvest in infra (Keima, Daruma) and acceleration.

## AI Leverage Strategy

- **Background coding agents** — async dev grunt work (paid IDE agents today; Daruma long-term — see [`DARUMA.md`](DARUMA.md))
- **Model-agnostic** — use cheapest capable provider each quarter (cloud APIs, local GPU, or both); no vendor lock-in in docs or workflow
- **Research assistants** — Claude, Gemini, others for architecture, debugging, Play Store research
- Reusable prompt library so research and building is repeatable
- Systematic idea scoring before building anything
- Templates cloned for every new app — never start from zero

Personal AI assistant vision, hardware, and phased infra: [`DARUMA.md`](DARUMA.md).
